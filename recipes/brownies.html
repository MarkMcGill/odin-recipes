Delicious Gooey Brownies



A fantastic treat for a cosy night in, or an epic night out!


It's such a quick an easy recipe, but even the fanciest Michelin Star chef will make these when they need a nice little treat.

Follow along, and make some of the best brownies you'll ever try!


Ingredients:

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 tablesppon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (Not essential)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
