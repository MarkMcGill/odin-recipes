Choco Chip Cookies



A fantastic nibble for a cosy night in, or an awesome night out!


It's such a fast an simple recipe, but even the fanciest TV Star chef's will make these when they need a nice little treat.

Follow along, and make some of the best cookies you'll ever taste!


Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (Optional)

Directions

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.

How long to bake the chocolate chip cookies?

In an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, the chocolate chip cookies should be perfectly baked in about 10 minutes. The edges should be golden brown and the cookies should be mostly set (they'll continue to set as the cool).
